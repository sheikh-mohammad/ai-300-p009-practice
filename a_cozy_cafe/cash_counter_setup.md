# Cozy Café Cash Counter Setup

## Overview
Design for a minimal, efficient cash counter that maintains the quiet, study-friendly atmosphere while ensuring smooth operations. The counter is designed to be functional without disrupting the calm ambiance of the café.

## Design Elements

### Counter Construction
- **Material**: Natural wood (light oak or birch) to match the furniture aesthetic
- **Height**: Standard 36" height with a 42" section for wheelchair accessibility
- **Depth**: 24" deep to provide adequate workspace without being imposing
- **Edge**: Rounded corners for safety and softness in appearance
- **Finish**: Matte, non-reflective surface to reduce visual distraction

### Colors & Aesthetics
- **Counter surface**: Light wood tone with subtle grain
- **Base**: Soft sage green or warm gray to complement the wooden top
- **Accents**: Minimal brass or bronze fixtures for a warm touch
- **No harsh or bright colors** that would contrast with the study-friendly environment

### Lighting
- **Counter lighting**: Warm LED strips (2700K-3000K) under the counter edge
- **POS area**: Soft task lighting focused on the register area
- **Menu display**: Gentle backlighting for visibility without glare
- **Ambient lighting**: Integrated with the overall café lighting scheme

## Functional Components

### Point of Sale (POS) Station
- **Integrated tablet/smart system** to minimize visual clutter
- **Minimal hardware** (card reader, receipt printer only)
- **Positioned** to allow staff to maintain eye contact with customers
- **Screen orientation** allows customer to view order summary when needed

### Menu Display Area
- **Digital display** with soft, readable fonts
- **Backlit with warm light** for visibility without harshness
- **Positioned at eye level** for comfortable viewing
- **Simple, clean layout** with daily specials clearly marked

### Order Pickup Area
- **Designated section** clearly marked but not obtrusive
- **Small LED indicators** to identify orders (warm-toned lights)
- **Heating elements** for food items kept minimal and quiet
- **Width**: 18" to accommodate multiple orders without crowding

### Staff Work Area
- **Behind-counter space** for drink preparation
- **Integrated storage** for cups, lids, napkins within easy reach
- **Minimal equipment** to reduce noise and visual clutter
- **Power outlets** for necessary equipment, hidden from view

## Acoustic Considerations
- **Sound-dampening materials** under the counter surface
- **Rubber feet** on equipment to minimize vibration noise
- **Staff training area** marked for quiet operations
- **Equipment placement** to minimize noise toward seating areas

## Accessibility Features
- **Wheelchair accessible** section at 30" height with 27" knee clearance
- **Braille menu options** available upon request
- **Clear sightlines** for customers with mobility aids
- **Non-slip surface** treatment on the counter edge

## Technology Integration
- **Wireless charging stations** discretely integrated into the counter
- **QR code ordering** system integrated with the POS
- **Contactless payment** options prominently featured
- **Order tracking system** visible to customers without being distracting

## Staff Workflow Optimization
- **Logical layout** for efficient drink preparation
- **Storage zones** organized by frequency of use
- **Clear sightlines** to seating area for customer service
- **Quiet equipment** to maintain the study-friendly atmosphere

## Size Specifications
- **Total counter length**: 12 feet
- **POS station**: 4 feet
- **Menu display area**: 3 feet
- **Order pickup**: 2 feet
- **Prep area**: 3 feet

## Special Features
- **Minimal visual barriers** to maintain open feel
- **Integrated cable management** to prevent tripping hazards
- **Easy-to-clean surfaces** that maintain aesthetic appeal
- **Flexible layout** to accommodate special events or promotions
- **Sustainable materials** that align with eco-conscious values

## Integration with Café Atmosphere
- **Design elements** that complement the overall aesthetic
- **Colors** that blend with the soft, muted palette
- **Scale** appropriate for the 10-15 seat space
- **Functionality** that supports the quiet, study-friendly mission
