# Cozy Café Staff Schedule

## Overview
A thoughtfully designed staff schedule that maintains the quiet, study-friendly atmosphere while ensuring efficient operations. The schedule prioritizes consistency, minimal disruption to studying students, and optimal service during peak academic times.

## Staffing Philosophy
- **Minimal staff presence** during peak study hours to maintain quiet atmosphere
- **Consistent schedules** to build familiarity with regular customers
- **Flexible coverage** during academic calendar fluctuations
- **Quiet service approach** emphasizing non-verbal communication and efficiency

## Staff Positions

### Barista (Primary Role)
- **Duties**: Beverage preparation, basic food service, light cleaning
- **Skills required**: Coffee preparation, POS operation, customer service
- **Noise level**: Maintain whisper-quiet operations during prep

### Host/Hostess (Secondary Role)
- **Duties**: Greeting customers, seating management, light maintenance
- **Skills required**: Customer service, basic POS knowledge, organization
- **Focus**: Discrete service that doesn't disrupt studying

### Shift Supervisor (Lead Role)
- **Duties**: All barista duties plus inventory, scheduling, customer relations
- **Skills required**: All above plus leadership, problem-solving
- **Responsibility**: Maintaining café atmosphere and standards

## Schedule Structure

### Daily Schedule Template
```
Time        Monday-Thursday     Friday-Sunday
5:00 AM     Prep shift start    Prep shift start
6:00 AM     Open for business   Open for business
7:00 AM     2 staff on duty     3 staff on duty
9:00 AM     1 staff on duty     2 staff on duty
11:00 AM    2 staff on duty     3 staff on duty
1:00 PM     1 staff on duty     2 staff on duty
3:00 PM     2 staff on duty     2 staff on duty
5:00 PM     1 staff on duty     2 staff on duty
7:00 PM     Close prep          Close prep
8:00 PM     Close operations    Close operations
```

### Weekly Schedule (12-Seat Café)

#### Morning Shift (5:00 AM - 1:00 PM)
- **Monday-Thursday**: 2 staff (Barista + Shift Supervisor)
- **Friday-Sunday**: 3 staff (2 Baristas + Shift Supervisor)
- **Peak times**: 7:00-9:00 AM and 11:00 AM-1:00 PM

#### Afternoon Shift (11:00 AM - 7:00 PM)
- **Monday-Thursday**: 2 staff (Barista + Shift Supervisor)
- **Friday-Sunday**: 2-3 staff depending on demand
- **Peak times**: 12:00-2:00 PM (lunch) and 3:00-5:00 PM (study prep)

#### Closing Shift (5:00 PM - 8:00 PM)
- **Monday-Thursday**: 1 staff (Shift Supervisor)
- **Friday-Sunday**: 2 staff (Barista + Shift Supervisor)

## Academic Calendar Considerations

### Regular Semester
- **Monday-Thursday**: Higher traffic during traditional class hours
- **Friday-Sunday**: Consistent study traffic throughout
- **Evening hours**: Extended hours during midterms and finals

### Midterm/Finals Periods
- **Extended hours**: Open 24 hours with minimal staff
- **Quiet protocols**: Strict noise management during exam periods
- **Additional staff**: 25% increase during peak stress periods

### Break Periods
- **Reduced hours**: 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- **Minimal staff**: 1-2 staff depending on traffic
- **Maintenance focus**: Deep cleaning and preparation for next semester

## Staff Communication Protocols

### During Shift Changes
- **Quiet handover**: 15-minute transition period with minimal conversation
- **Status update**: Written notes only during customer hours
- **Equipment check**: Silent verification of all equipment

### Customer Interaction Guidelines
- **Greeting protocol**: Warm but quiet acknowledgment
- **Order taking**: Efficient and clear communication
- **Service delivery**: Minimal conversation unless requested
- **Problem resolution**: Discrete handling away from study areas

### Emergency Protocols
- **Medical emergency**: Designated staff member handles while others maintain atmosphere
- **Equipment failure**: Quick, quiet resolution with backup plans
- **Disruptive customer**: Polite but firm intervention

## Shift Rotation System

### Weekly Rotation
- **Consistent assignments** for familiarity and efficiency
- **Cross-training** to ensure coverage flexibility
- **Preference consideration** for academic staff needs

### Monthly Schedule Distribution
- **Posted by 25th** of previous month
- **Digital format** to reduce paper noise
- **Clear shift swaps** with supervisor approval

### Conflict Resolution

## Compensation & Benefits
- **Competitive wages** to ensure quality staff retention
- **Flexible scheduling** to accommodate student staff needs
- **Study breaks** during slow periods
- **Free beverages** during shifts
- **End-of-semester bonuses** for consistent performance

## Special Considerations

### Peak Academic Times
- **Midterm weeks**: Additional staff for extended hours
- **Finals week**: 24-hour operation with minimal disruption
- **Assignment due dates**: Extended evening hours

### Quiet Protocols
- **Prep work**: All grinding, cleaning during non-peak hours
- **Equipment maintenance**: Scheduled during slow periods
- **Staff breaks**: Away from customer areas

### Seasonal Adjustments
- **Summer**: Reduced hours with core staff
- **Back-to-school**: Intensive training for new staff
- **Winter break**: Minimal operation with skeleton crew

## Quality Metrics
- **Customer feedback**: Anonymous suggestion box
- **Atmosphere monitoring**: Regular assessment of noise levels
- **Efficiency tracking**: Wait times and service quality
- **Staff satisfaction**: Regular check-ins for retention
