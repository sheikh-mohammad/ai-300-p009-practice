# Cozy Café Seating Layout

## Overview
Design for a quiet, study-friendly café with 12 seats total, following the constraints of soft colors, wooden furniture, and warm lighting.

## Layout Design

```
    [ENTRANCE]
        |
    [COUNTER]
        |
    ┌─────────────────────────────────┐
    │  ┌─┬─┐   ┌─┬─┐   ┌─┬─┐   ┌─┬─┐  │
    │  │W│W│   │W│W│   │W│W│   │W│W│  │ ← Study Tables (4-person)
    │  └─┴─┘   └─┴─┘   └─┴─┘   └─┴─┘  │
    │                               │
    │  ┌─────┐           ┌─────┐    │
    │  │  S  │           │  S  │    │ ← Sofa Seating (2-person)
    │  │  2  │           │  2  │    │
    │  └─────┘           └─────┘    │
    │                               │
    │  ┌─┬─┬─┐         ┌─┬─┬─┐      │
    │  │W│W│W│         │W│W│W│      │ ← Study Tables (3-person)
    │  └─┴─┴─┘         └─┴─┴─┘      │
    │                               │
    └─────────────────────────────────┘

    W = Wooden study desk with chair
    S = Wooden sofa with table
```

## Seating Details

### Study Tables (6 seats)
- **4 two-person study tables** with wooden tops and warm-toned cushions
- Each table has **separate workspaces** to ensure individual focus
- Positioned with adequate spacing for quiet concentration
- Soft lighting (warm LED pendants) above each table

### Sofa Seating (4 seats)
- **2 two-person wooden sofas** with soft, muted-colored cushions
- Paired with small wooden side tables
- Located in quieter corners of the café
- Ambient warm lighting to create a cozy atmosphere

### Group Study Tables (3 seats)
- **2 three-person study tables** for small collaborative groups
- Wooden construction with individual task lighting
- Positioned to allow quiet discussion without disturbing solo studiers
- Warm lighting focused on work surfaces

## Design Elements

### Furniture
- All furniture made of **natural wood** (light oak or birch)
- Chairs with **ergonomic support** and soft cushions in muted tones
- Tables with **adequate surface space** for laptops, books, and study materials

### Colors
- **Soft, muted color palette**: cream, light beige, warm grays, muted sage green
- Wooden tones providing natural warmth
- No bright or harsh colors that could distract from studying

### Lighting
- **Warm lighting** throughout (2700K-3000K temperature)
- Individual table lamps on study tables
- Wall sconces providing ambient lighting
- No bright white lights as per project constraints

### Acoustic Considerations
- **Sound-absorbing materials** to maintain quiet atmosphere
- Wooden furniture with fabric cushions to dampen sound
- Strategic placement of seating to separate quiet zones from slightly more social areas

## Total Capacity: 12 Seats
- 8 seats at individual study tables
- 4 seats at sofa seating
- Within the required 10-15 seat constraint

## Special Features
- **Power outlets** available at each study station
- **Quiet zones** clearly defined for individual study
- **Light reading areas** near windows for natural light during daytime
- **Minimal visual barriers** to maintain open, welcoming feel while respecting study needs
