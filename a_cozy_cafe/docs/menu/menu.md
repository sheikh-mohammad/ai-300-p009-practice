# Cozy Café Menu

## Study-Friendly Features
- **Noisy food alert**: Items that are particularly crunchy or loud to eat are clearly marked
- **Stain-resistant options**: Spill-resistant packaging for long study sessions
- **Sustained energy focus**: Menu items designed to provide steady energy without crashes
- **Temperature retention**: Drinks served in quality cups that keep beverages at optimal temperature longer

## Pricing Strategy
- All prices designed to be student-friendly
- Study session bundles: Any drink + pastry for $5.00 discount
- Loyalty program: 10th drink free

## Special Accommodations
- Dairy-free and plant-based milk alternatives available (+$0.50)
- Gluten-free options clearly marked
- Nut-free preparation area available upon request
- Decaf options for late-night study sessions

## Menu Design Philosophy
- Simple, easy-to-read typography for comfortable reading
- Soft color scheme matching café interior
- Minimal text to avoid visual clutter while studying
- Clear pricing to facilitate quick ordering
- Seasonal rotation of special items to maintain interest
